How to Get Started with AI

This guide will walk you through everything you need to begin your journey in Artificial Intelligence.

  • ✅ Learn the basics of Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • ✅ Take free online courses (Coursera, YouTube, etc.)
  • ✅ Play with tools like ChatGPT, Google Teachable Machine
  • ✅ Build beginner projects (like a chatbot or image classifier)
  • ✅ Join AI communities on Reddit or GitHub
